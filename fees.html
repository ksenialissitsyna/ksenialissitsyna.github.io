Flex Spending

Therapy sessions are 100% covered by your flex spending or health spending accounts. I can provide you with a receipt that you can submit for reimbursement. Energy work and past life regression sessions are not eligible for FSA reimbursement at this time.

Payment

Payment must be made at the beginning of each session. I accept cash, checks, and Paypal. If you choose to do Paypal, payments are due prior to our first appointment.

Cancellation Policy

I have a 24 hour cancellation policy with the exception of emergency situations. If you cancel an appointment within 24 hours, or if you are a no-show for any reason, you are responsible for that fee.
